new module:extras/torusknot.TorusKnot(revolutions, r, q, s1, m1)

A curve evaluator object for a torus knot or torus-knot-like curve. Uses the formula given in "Decorative Knot Patterns" by L. D. Taylor, 2011.

Parameters

  • revolutions (Type: number)
    Integer greater than 0. Labeled 'p' by Taylor.
  • r (Type: Array.<number>)
    Array of integers and/or nonintegers.
  • q (Type: Array.<number>)
    Array of integers. Lowest 'q' is the number of equal "parts" of the torus knot.
  • s1 (Type: number)
    Integer or noninteger parameter.
  • m1 (Type: number)
    Integer parameter.
